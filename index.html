Docking tips

W and S are single clean strokes, so tap them to move. A and D also fire only one single one-sided stroke per press.

Use Z or C for backward correction, and alternate taps rather than holding A or D for steering.

Goal: Park in the white box by the bridge with the boat at 355° to 5° relative to the bridge.

Park by the bridge

Approach from open water, slow down, rotate the shell, and settle fully into the bridge-side parking box.
